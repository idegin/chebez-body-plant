**Version:** 1.0
**Industry:** Food & Beverage (Natural & Plant-Based Products)
**Business Model:** B2B Distribution (Primary) with B2C Brand Awareness

---

# Website Objective

The website should position Bodyplant Nature Resources as a premium Nigerian manufacturer and distributor of healthy plant-based products while primarily generating wholesale enquiries from:

* Supermarkets
* Pharmacies
* Grocery chains
* Distributors
* Hotels
* Restaurants
* Health stores
* Bulk buyers

The website should not function as an eCommerce store.

Instead, it should become a sales and credibility platform that encourages wholesale enquiries, partnership applications, and retailer onboarding.

# Content Strategy

The website should balance **brand storytelling**, **educational health content**, and **commercial distribution messaging**. Rather than functioning as a simple digital brochure, it should position Bodyplant as a trusted manufacturing partner with premium, certified products. Every page should reinforce three core themes:

* **Natural & Healthy:** 100% plant-based, wholesome nutrition for everyday living.
* **Trusted & Certified:** Quality manufacturing backed by NAFDAC, SON, and HALAL certifications.
* **Built for Business:** Reliable production, consistent supply, and long-term wholesale partnerships for distributors and retailers.

This structure creates a professional, scalable website that builds consumer trust while optimizing conversions from wholesalers, supermarkets, pharmacies, and other retail partners.

---

# Conversion Strategy & Measurement

The site is a lead engine, not a brochure. Every page drives toward one primary macro-conversion — **Become a Distributor** — supported by lower-friction micro-conversions.

## Conversion hierarchy

1. **Primary (macro):** Distributor application submitted.
2. **Secondary:** Wholesale price-list request · Speak to Sales (call/WhatsApp) · Catalogue download (email capture).
3. **Micro:** Newsletter signup · product enquiry · scroll-depth & CTA engagement.

## Funnel & page intent

| Stage | Intent | Pages | Primary CTA |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Attract | SEO + brand discovery | Home, SEO landing pages, Products | Explore Products |
| Trust | Prove certification & quality | About, Manufacturing, Certifications | Learn More |
| Convert | Turn intent into a lead | Distribution, Become a Distributor | Become a Distributor |
| Support | Reduce friction / objections | FAQs, Contact | Speak to Sales |

## Persistent conversion elements

Sticky "Become a Distributor" (desktop), floating WhatsApp (pulse), mobile call button, persistent certification badges on product pages, exit-intent catalogue offer.

## Success metrics (KPIs)

- **Primary:** distributor applications / month; application form completion rate.
- **Secondary:** price-list requests, catalogue downloads, Sales calls initiated.
- **Engagement:** LCP < 2.5s, CLS < 0.1, INP < 200ms; bounce, scroll-depth to CTA, form field drop-off.
- **SEO:** rankings for the target queries listed above; organic → lead conversion rate.

## Trust & credibility levers (used site-wide)

Certifications (NAFDAC · SON · HALAL), transparent ingredient/sourcing story, consistent premium visual system, real photography over stock, and social proof (retailer/distributor testimonials as they mature).
